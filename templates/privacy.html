Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use AlohaAgent, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address and password (hashed)
  • Profile information: full name, brokerage, island, license number (optional)
  • Usage data: form inputs you submit and AI-generated outputs
  • Technical data: browser type, IP address, session cookies

2. How We Use It

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the AlohaAgent service
  • Save and display your generation history on your dashboard
  • Enforce usage limits and plan restrictions
  • Send service-related communications (no marketing without consent)
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve AI outputs

3. Data Storage

Your account data and generation history are stored in a secured database. Passwords are stored as one-way bcrypt hashes and are never readable. We retain generation history as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

AlohaAgent uses the Anthropic API to generate AI content. When you submit a form, the property details and inputs you provide are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Hawaii License Numbers

If you provide your Hawaii real estate license number in your profile, it is stored securely and is never shared with third parties, displayed publicly, or used for any purpose other than auto-filling your tool forms to save time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@alohaagent.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Contact

Privacy questions or concerns? Email hello@alohaagent.com. AlohaAgent is operated by AlohaForge.
